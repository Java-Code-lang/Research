  • Feudalism (800s–1400s): Land-based wealth; limited trade.
  • Trade & Merchants (1400s–1600s): Rise of merchants, banking, joint-stock companies.
  • Industrial Revolution (1700s–1800s): Factories, wage labor, mass production.
  • Finance & Monopolies (1800s–1900s): Banks, corporations, colonial resources.
  • 20th Century: U.S.-led global capitalism, Bretton Woods, corporations.
  • Today: Tech, AI, data-driven; Amazon, Google, Meta dominate.
